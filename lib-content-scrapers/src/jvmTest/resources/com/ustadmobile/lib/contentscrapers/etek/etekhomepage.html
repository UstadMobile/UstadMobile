Core subjects definitions

Items in the eTekkatho library are assigned to one or more subject headings and sub-headings. The table below shows you how each subject is defined. These definitions are designed to help you explore the library and find the resources you need. You can click on the subject heading to drill down through the sub-headings to the individual resources.

As we continue to build the eTekkatho library, the coverage within each of these subject headings will increase and additional sub-headings may be added. Coverage exists for all the subject areas shown below.

Subjects and their definitions by subject area for core subjects.
Subject heading Sub-heading Definition
Education Educational assessment Measurement and documentation of knowledge, skills, attitudes and beliefs within the educational setting
Educational policy Rules and guidelines for educational systems, educational standards, school inspections and the effectiveness of educational policy
Educational technology Use of technology in the classroom, elearning, learning technology and its educational applications
Earth sciences Climatology * The study of climate including climate systems, climate change, climate records, climate patterns and indices, and climate modelling

Items marked with an asterisk (*) are included in both Earth sciences and Physical geography.

eTekkatho hotspots

We have installed the eTekkatho library on the local area networks of our partnering institutions across Myanmar. These are "hotspots" this means if you are in the building at a partner university, you can access the full eTekkatho digital library, including our Special Collections, wirelessly at a broadband speed.
